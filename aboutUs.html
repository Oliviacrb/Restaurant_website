Welcome to the Restaurant

Our story

Our restaurant was founded by Nancy Drew in 1943. The first restaurant site was on the 1st floor of a public house with a small kitchen. This is where the story begins...

Authentic home-cooking style

Nancy always wanted the restaurant experience to feel like coming home. She placed huge importance on authentic local recipes and dishes, but served for the community.

Present Day

We are proud to continue cooking with innovative ingredients across the chain of 5 restaurants, while retaining Nancy's original recipes and home-cooking style
