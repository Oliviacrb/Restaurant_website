Welcome to Keller Taproom!

Here at The Keller Taproom, flavour takes centre-stage. We source the best British produce from coast and countryside, cooking elements over coals and wood to give our food a unique edge.

Check out our current menu to see how we subtly incorporate charring, smoking and fire roasting in our legendary burgers and seasonal dishes, as well as our famous Sunday Lunches and indulgent evening meals.

At its heart The Packet is a great local pub, serving food from the surrounding fields and coast alongside hand-crafted beer.

Our philosophy of cooking over live fire perfectly complements the raw simplicity we strive for: great food, drink and hospitality.

Here are the awards we have won so far:

  • Camra Real Ale Award 2022
  • Good Food Guide Winner 2019
  • Edinburgh Pub of the year 2022
  • Top 5 Pub in Edinburgh on Tripadvisor
